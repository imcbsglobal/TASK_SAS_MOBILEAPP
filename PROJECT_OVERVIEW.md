# TaskPrime SAS: User Guide & Feature Overview

**TaskPrime SAS** is a complete mobile solution for sales teams and retail businesses. It is designed to help you manage your daily sales, orders, and collections easily, even when you don't have an internet connection.

---

## 🌟 Key Benefits

- **Work Anywhere**: Take orders and collect payments even in remote areas without internet.
- **Save Time**: No more manual paperwork. Everything is recorded digitally and synced to your office automatically.
- **Accurate Records**: Keep track of every customer's balance, every product's stock, and every payment made.
- **Professionalism**: Print professional receipts on the spot or share them instantly via WhatsApp/Email.

---

## 📱 Main Features

### 1. Daily Sales & Orders
- **Quick Order Entry**: Select products, adjust quantities, and save orders in seconds.
- **Smart Search**: Find any product by name, code, or by scanning its barcode with your phone's camera.
- **Inventory Tracking**: Always know what's in stock. See different batches, prices, and expiry dates for each item.

### 2. Customer Management & Collections
- **Full Customer List**: Access your entire customer database with their phone numbers, locations, and current balances.
- **Easy Collections**: Record payments settled by Cash or Cheque.
- **Customer Ledger**: View a complete history of a customer's transactions to resolve any disputes instantly.

### 3. Field Tools
- **Attendance (Punch-In)**: Start your workday by "Punching In" to record your time and location.
- **Location Capture**: Save the exact GPS location of your customers to help with deliveries and future visits.
- **Reports**: See your performance at a glance—total sales, pending collections, and daily activity.

### 4. Printing & Sharing
- **Instant Receipts**: Connect to a small Bluetooth printer to give your customers a printed receipt immediately.
- **Digital Sharing**: Create a PDF version of any bill and share it directly via WhatsApp.

---

## 🔄 How to Use the App (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Start Your Day
1.  **Login**: Enter your username and password.
2.  **Sync Data**: On the Home screen, click **"Download Data"**. This brings all the latest prices, products, and customer balances from the office to your phone.
3.  **Punch-In**: Mark your attendance to start your work.

### Step 2: During Customer Visits
1.  **Find Customer**: Search for the customer you are visiting.
2.  **Create Order**: If they want to buy something, enter a new Order. The app will save it even if you are offline.
3.  **Collect Payment**: If they pay you, enter a new Collection.
4.  **Print/Share**: Give them a receipt (printed or digital).

### Step 3: End of Day (Syncing)
1.  **Reconnect**: When you have internet, go to the Home screen.
2.  **Sync Now**: Click the **"Sync Now"** button. This sends all your offline orders and collections to the office systems.
3.  **Log Out**: Safely log out to protect your account.

---

## 📝 Important Notes for Users

- **Internet Requirement**: You only need internet to **Download Data** in the morning and **Sync Now** at the end of the day. You can use all other features offline.
- **License**: The app will show a message if your license is about to expire. Please contact your administrator if you see a "Demo Expired" or "License Expired" message.
- **Battery & Location**: Using GPS and Bluetooth printing uses more battery. It's recommended to carry a portable charger for long field days.
